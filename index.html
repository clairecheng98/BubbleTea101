My Favorite Milk Tea Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Tea bags * 3 or loose leaf tea
  • 300ml whole milk
  • Sugar
  • Condensed milk

Steps:

  1. Boil the tea in 50ml water on high heat till boiling.
  2. Add in whole milk, keep on medium heat for 3-5 min.
  3. Add suger and condensed milk based on preference.
  4. Filter out the tea leaves. And it is ready for serve!
